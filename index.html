How it works

Our simple 5‑step process keeps you informed at every stage, from first request to cargo arrival at your chosen port.

Step 1
Request
You send product details (type, brand, year, budget, destination) via the form, email or WhatsApp.
Step 2
Sourcing
We consult our European network and share available offers with prices, photos and lead times.
Step 3
Agreement
You choose an offer; we define our service fee and responsibilities in a written sourcing mandate.
Step 4–5
Purchase & shipping
We coordinate payment with the supplier, book freight to Douala or another port, and provide all shipping documents.
